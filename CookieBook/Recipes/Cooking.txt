Title: Beef Noodles
Ingredients:
- 1 Fantastic 2 minute noodles
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1/8 cup milk
Cooking Time: 5 minutes
Instructions:
- Crush the noodles into a small pot
- Add the beef flavouring and fill the pot with water, stopping when it's just below the top of the noodles.
- Add the milk and butter and cook on medium heat until thick and most of the liquid is gone.
- Allow to cool for a minute before consuming

Title: Eggless Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
- 1 tsp Sugar
- 2 tsp Baking Powder
- 1/4 tsp Salt
- 1 and 1/8 Cup Milk
- 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
- 1 tbsp Water
- 1 tsp Vanilla Extracts
- 2 tbsp Butter
Cooking Time: 5 to 7 minutes per cake
Instructions:
- Whisk together the dry ingredients.
- Measure the milk in a measuring jug and add the oil, water and vanilla extract
- Mix and wet and dry ingredients together, ensuring not to overmix
- Add the butter to a frying pan and leave to melt
- Once the butter is melted, add it to the rest of the pancake batter and mix (You do not have to get it all as butter is needed in the frying pan)
- When the butter coating in the frying pan is slightly bubbling, pour a ladleful of batter on the pan
- Once the majority of the bubbles on the pancake have popped, flip and continue to cook until golden brown

Title: Traditional Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 2 Cup All Purpose Flour
- 2 tsp Baking Powder
- 1/2 tsp Salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 Cup Sugar
- 1 and 1/2 Cup Milk, approx.
- 1 tsp Vanilla Extracts
- Butter
Cooking Time: 5 to 7 minutes per cake
Instructions:
- Sift the flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl.
- In another bowl beat the eggs and sugar with a whisk until pale and thick.
- Add the egg mixture and the milk to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
- Heat a non-stick frying pan and add a small teaspon of butter
- Add a ladleful of btter to the pan
- Once the majority of the bubbles on the pancake have popped, flip and continue to cook until golden brown
