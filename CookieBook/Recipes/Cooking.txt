Title: Beef Noodles
Ingredients:
- 1 Fantastic 2 minute noodles
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1/8 cup milk
Cooking Time: 5 minutes
Instructions: 
- Crush the noodles into a small pot
- Add the beef flavouring and fill the pot with water, stopping when it's just below the top of the noodles.
- Add the milk and butter and cook on medium heat until thick and most of the liquid is gone.
- Allow to cool for a minute before consuming
