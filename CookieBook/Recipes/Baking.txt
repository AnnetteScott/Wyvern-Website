Title: Baking Recipe
Ingredients:
- 2 Cup All Purpose Flours
- 2 tsp Baking Powder
- 1/2 tsp Salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 Cup Sugar
- 1 and 1/2 Cup Milk, approx.
- 1 tsp Vanilla Extracts
- Butter
Cooking Time: 5 to 7 minutes per cake
Instructions:
- Sift the flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl.
- In another bowl beat the eggs and sugar with a whisk until pale and thick.
- Add the egg mixture and the milk to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
- Heat a non-stick frying pan and add a small teaspon of butter
- Add a ladleful of btter to the pan
- Once the majority of the bubbles on the pancake have popped, flip and continue to cook until golden brown
